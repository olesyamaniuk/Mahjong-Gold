FAQ

  • What is Mahjong Gold - Treasure Trail?
    Mahjong Gold - Treasure Trail is an adventure-themed tile-matching puzzle game where players solve mahjong boards to unlock hidden treasures and explore ancient mysteries.
  • Is the game free to play?
    Yes! The game is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases for additional boosters and customization options.
  • How do I progress in the game?
    Complete levels by matching identical tiles and clearing the board. Advancing through the game unlocks new maps, treasure chests, and special power-ups.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes! You can enjoy the game offline, but some features like events and daily rewards require an internet connection.
  • What are in-app purchases used for?
    In-app purchases allow players to buy boosters, additional tile sets, and exclusive adventure packs to enhance their gaming experience.
  • Are there daily rewards?
    Absolutely! Log in daily to claim rewards, unlock exclusive challenges, and earn bonus power-ups.
